About News Comparator

News outlets lean politically based on which values, policies, and interpretations they tend to emphasise. It does not mean the reporting is false — it means the framing, selection of quotes, and emphasis may differ.News Comparator fetches the same story from multiple sources and shows how differently it is reported based on political leaning — so you can form a more complete picture.

Left Perspectives: Focuses on equality, government role, worker & minority rights, environmental protections.
Center Perspectives: Aims to report facts with minimal political framing. Focuses heavily on direct quotes and objective events.
Right Perspectives: Focuses on free markets, tradition, national security, individual liberty, and less regulation.
